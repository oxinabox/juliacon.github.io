Julia Community Prize

We wish to recognise the work of our amazing community, and will award three prizes for outstanding contributions to the Julia ecosystem. These prizes will come with a cash award of USD 1000 each and certificate.

Please nominate your favourite Julia contributor (or yourself) for this award. Contributions can be in any area - code, packages, documentation, applications, or outreach. Contributions can also have been over any period.

The awards will be announced and presented during JuliaCon 2018 in London.

Nominations

Nominations (including self nominations) can be made via this form. Anyone can be nominated, except the four Julia founders, and the members of the award committee. All nominations will be considered confidentially.

Nominations will be accepted until the end of Friday, the 13th of July, anywhere on earth.

Awards Committee

The nominations will be considered, and winner decided, by a comittee comprised of emiment members of the Julia community, with representations from both academia and industry. All decisions of the awards committee will be final.

The members of the committee for 2018 are Prof Alan Edelman, MIT; Prof Tim Holy, WU; and Mr Curtis Vogt, Invenia.
